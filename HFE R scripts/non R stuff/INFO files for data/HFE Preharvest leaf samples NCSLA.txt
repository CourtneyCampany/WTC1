
Before final harvest (on 2009-3-11), a small sample of leaves was taken from each layer (sunlit/shaded) for chemical analyses etc.

For N and C analyses: Analysed by Roslyn Woodfield using LECO TruSpec Micro. Standard used - Oatmeal (%N 2.70+/- 0.04, %C 46.85+/-0.4).

layer	- 1-5, counting from bottom layer.
position - Inner or outer crown section
leafarea - Total leaf area (cm2)
freshweight	- Fresh weight (g)
dryweight - Dry weight (g)
SLA	- Specific leaf area (cm g-1)
nperc - nitrogen concentration (%)
cperc - carbon concentration (%)

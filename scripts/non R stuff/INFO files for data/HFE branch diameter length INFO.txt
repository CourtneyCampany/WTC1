
All branch census data. Diameters and lengths of all branches on the chamber trees were measured 7 times
between 2008-4-15 and 2009-3-16.

Data were thoroughly checked for consistency across dates (i.e. branch numbers, diameters and lengths),
many fixes were made (noted in the comments field).
